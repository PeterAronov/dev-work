Dr. Elena Petrova (elena.petrova@neuralx.ai) is an AI research scientist based in Zurich, Switzerland. 
She holds a Ph.D. in Machine Learning from ETH Zurich, where her dissertation focused on transformer interpretability and multilingual embeddings. 
Elena has published papers at NeurIPS and ICML and is currently leading a research group at NeuralX AI Labs, where she focuses on foundation models, federated learning, and low-resource language models. 
She has collaborated with Hugging Face and DeepMind on multilingual benchmark development. 
Elena's skills include PyTorch, Hugging Face Transformers, TensorFlow, and fastai. 
She speaks five languages fluently, mentors early-career female researchers, and is an advocate for open science and ethical AI.
